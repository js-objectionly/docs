# Welcome to Objectionly

Objectionly helps teams turn sales calls into searchable, coachable workspace data.
Start by setting up who can access the workspace, which reps should be tracked,
and which meetings should be imported for analysis.

<Callout icon="user-shield">
  Most setup steps require an Admin. Members can view reporting for the reps
  they have access to.
</Callout>

## Get started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign in to the dashboard">
    Go to [app.objectionly.com](https://app.objectionly.com/) to create an
    account or sign in.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set up your team">
    Add the reps whose calls should be tracked, invite workspace members, and
    assign the right role for each teammate.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect your call source">
    Choose the integration that matches where your calls live. Objectionly can
    import calls from conferencing tools, dialers, CRMs, and Zapier workflows.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Meeting Filters">
    Use title, rep, and duration filters to avoid processing internal meetings,
    test calls, and conversations that are too short to score.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review processed calls">
    After calls import, use Objectionly to review transcripts, scores, and
    reporting by rep or team.
  </Step>
</Steps>
