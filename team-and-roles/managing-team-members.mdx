title: Managing Team Members
description: Invite teammates to Objectionly and manage login roles for your workspace.
---

Invite teammates to Objectionly so they can log in and view workspace data. Members and Admins are the two login roles.

<Callout icon="user-shield">
  **Who can do this:** Admins
</Callout>

## In this section

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Invite a Member" icon="user-plus" href="/team-and-roles/invite-a-member">
    Invite someone who needs read access without workspace management controls.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Invite an Admin" icon="shield-plus" href="/team-and-roles/invite-an-admin">
    Invite someone who needs full workspace management access.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Change a Role" icon="repeat" href="/team-and-roles/change-a-role">
    Promote a Member to Admin or demote an Admin to Member.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Remove a Member" icon="user-minus" href="/team-and-roles/remove-a-member">
    Remove someone's login access from your workspace.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
