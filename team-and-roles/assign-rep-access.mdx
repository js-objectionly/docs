<Callout icon="user-shield">
  **Who can do this:** Admins
</Callout>

Change which reps a Member can view anytime.

## Assign rep access

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the Team page">
    <a href="https://app.objectionly.com/dashboard/team" className="inline-flex items-center rounded-lg bg-blue-600 px-3 py-2 text-sm font-medium text-white no-underline hover:bg-blue-700" style={{ color: "#FFFFFF" }}>Open Team page</a>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the Member">
    Choose the Member whose rep access you want to update.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open rep access settings">
    Open the Member's **rep access** settings.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose an access type">
    Choose **all reps**, **specific reps**, or **none**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick specific reps, if needed">
    If you choose specific reps, select the reps from the list.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save the change">
    Changes take effect immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>
